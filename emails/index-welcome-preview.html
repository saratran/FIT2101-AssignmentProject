Welcome to DevAlarm, {{name}}!

Your life is about to get a whole lot more notified with DevAlarm. From now on, you will receive customisable updates whenever a change has been made to a file you have contributed to, or created from your repositories.

Getting Started

View Your Repositories

Go to your dashboard and view a list of all your repositories. Here you can change which repositories to be notified for, and customize the frequency at which those notifications are delivered.

 

See File Statistics

See your files in a new way with contribution ratios, line change counts per contributor, and all contributor contact information in one place.

 

See All Files and Unresolved Issues

See a list of all files within your repositories as well as all open issues in one place!
